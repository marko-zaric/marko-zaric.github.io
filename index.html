Hello!

My name is Marko Zarić and I am passionate about everything Robot Learning.

Currently I am doing my PhD at the Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems in Tübingen under the guidance of my two brilliant Advisors Michael Muehlebach and Claire Vernade.

My work current work lies at the intersection of Reinforcement Learning and Control, developing new Algorithms that perform efficienctly in real world online settings.

Before my PhD studies I was a student researcher at the Intelligent and Interactive Systems Group in the University of Innsbruck working on effect-driven action prototype learning in robotics. I also have +4 years industry experience working as a Software Devoloper (Storetec Systems GmbH), Data Scientist (BE-terna GmbH) and Digital Signal Processing Engineer (Tesat-Spacecom GmbH & Co. KG).
